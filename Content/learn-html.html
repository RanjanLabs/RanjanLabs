Common Tags Explained

<h1> to <h6> (Heading): Used for titles and section headings. <h1> is the most important (largest), and <h6> is the least. Headings are crucial for structure.
<p> (Paragraph): Used for a block of standard text or paragraph. Browsers automatically add some space above and below it.
<strong> (Strong Importance): Used to indicate that its content is of strong importance, seriousness, or urgency. It is typically rendered as **bold text**.
